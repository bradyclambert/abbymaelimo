Abby Mae's Limousine Service, LLC is a family-run business that was created in October of 1995. We live in, and our business is located in the city of Brockton, Massachusetts. We provide service throughout the Boston area and southeastern Massachusetts. We are Massport and US DOT approved and insured. We are a member of both the National Limousine Association and the New England Livery Association. Our staff includes a number of full and part-time employees committed to providing quality service. Phone lines are answered by our own knowledgeable employees from 9:00 am to 9:00 pm daily. We feature clean, comfortable, non-smoking vehicles for any occasion. Our top three priorties are: courtesy, reliability, and punctuality. References can always be furnished upon request! Remember, "we're large enough to serve you, but small enough to give the personal attention you deserve!"
